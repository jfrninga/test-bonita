What is Bonita Camp?

It's a free introductory session to the Bonita platform. Using the Bonita
Community Edition gain both theoretical knowledge and hands-on
experience to help build your first Bonita application.

Bonita Camp video series consists of 10 short chapters to get you
familiarized with concepts and key features of Bonita.

In under 60 minutes learn all the basics of Bonita.

Why Bonita Camp?

Bonita comes with lots of components and you need a basic
understanding of them in order to proceed.
To help you get started with Bonita, we have created the Bonita
Camp Video Series.

With 10 Chapters and being- just under 60 minutes, you will
learn all the essentials to create your first Bonita application

For Whom?

Bonita Camp is for anyone wanting to learn more about Bonita.
Whether you are a developer by profession or just technically inclined
and looking to develop business process applications - Bonita Camp
is designed to provide you with everything you need to create your
first project with Bonita

No matter your technical level, Bonita camp will offer a useful
overview of the Bonita platform.
